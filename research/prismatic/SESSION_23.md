### Compositions

### Interpretation

**Σ operators (pure rotation)** have **orders as powers of 2** (16 = 2⁴, 32 = 2⁵). Natural — these are unipotent operators I + N in char 2, so M^{2^m} = I + N^{2^m} = I when 2^m ≥ nilpotency index.

**σ operators (with SHR)** are NOT periodic in simple way. Order exceeds 256. Reflects their non-unipotent nature (Session 19).

**Composition structure**: Σ_0 ∘ Σ_1 still has order 32 (same as single Σ). But once σ joins composition, periodicity breaks.

## Suggestive numerical coincidence — Wang-barrier

**SHA's Wang-barrier (methodology T_BARRIER_EQUALS_SCHEDULE) is at r=17.** This means:
- Wang-chain deterministically controls e-register for rounds 2..16 (15 rounds)
- At r=17, schedule derivation W[16] = σ_1(W[14]) + W[9] + σ_0(W[1]) + W[0] breaks freedom
- r=17 = 16 + 1

**Our computation**: **Σ_1 has order exactly 16** on F_2^{32}.

**Numerical coincidence**:
- 16 = Σ_1's cycling period
- 16 = rounds of Wang-chain deterministic control
- 17 = 16 + 1 = first round of derived schedule

**But proper interpretation**: Wang-barrier 16 is about **schedule freedom** (W[0..15] free, W[16+] derived). Our Σ_1 order 16 is about **single-operator cycling**. These are different phenomena mathematically. The numerical coincidence is likely **coincidental, not causal**.

However, **both are driven by SHA's design choice of 16-word message**, so there may be indirect structural connection. Specialist analysis required.
